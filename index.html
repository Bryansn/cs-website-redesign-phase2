Welcome to the CPP Computer Science Department

Empowering students through innovation, research, and industry-focused curriculum.

Welcome!

The Computer Science Department at Cal Poly Pomona offers both undergraduate and graduate programs with a strong emphasis on hands-on software design, applied computing, and real-world problem solving.

Students build a strong foundation in algorithms, programming languages, architecture, and systems, while also exploring advanced areas like cybersecurity, cloud computing, data science, artificial intelligence, mobile and web development, and game programming.

Our graduates are prepared for careers in software engineering, research, data analytics, and advanced study in computing disciplines.
