Minor in Data Science

Data Science Program

The Data Science minor allows students from any major to develop skills in data analysis, statistics, and computational methods. The program focuses on applying data science techniques to real-world problems across disciplines.

Students interested in the Data Science minor should review the program requirements in the University Catalog and be aware that, due to limited seats, enrollment may involve a waitlist.
