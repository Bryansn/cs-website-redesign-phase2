Minor in Computer Science

The Computer Science minor provides students from any major with a strong foundation in programming, algorithms, and core computing concepts. All lower-division CS courses are open to non-CS majors as long as prerequisites are met.

How to Declare the CS Minor

To declare the Computer Science minor, students should speak with their academic advisor and submit the appropriate university minor declaration form. The CS Department office can also provide additional guidance.
